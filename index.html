Our Story


Jason Kitchen, owner of Newlife Landscaping, has spent most of his life working in the landscape world. His early work experience included lawn care, golf course maintenance, garden and nursery, pool operations, and masonry.

In college Jason studied Business and Professional Sales. After several years working as a business manager for a tech company, an unexpected opportunity came to follow a dream and start a business. Reviving old work habits and having a passion to transform outdoor living spaces led to the birth of Newlife Landscaping.

Because we understand the biggest industry frustrations, we take pride in our ability to respond in a timely manner, communicate thoroughly, and provide value without sacrificing quality. We possess the right knowledge, work ethic, and equipment to get the job done right. Our customer market is very flexible ranging from small to large custom residential. Contact us to learn how we can help with your outdoor projects.

Jason Kitchen
Owner
